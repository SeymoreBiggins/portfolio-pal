About Me

Zachary Harris

As the first of Generation X, technology is something I’m very comfortable with. Life experience aside, I’m competent in over twelve different programming languages and even proficient with a few. I list Googling amongst my many skills. When I was young, I broke every pc game in my library just to troubleshoot them back to life, and this is a skill I’ve added to my repertoire as a programmer as well.

My family consists of my wife, our three dogs, and our numerous plants. In my personal life I enjoy Olympic weightlifting, fermentation, and indoor hydroponics. For media, I enjoy video essays on video game ethics, dissections of decades old games, and watching programmers engineer physics simulations.

My guiding design principle is simplicity. If an idea cannot be conveyed in a simple to understand fashion, perhaps its time to go back to the drawing board.

Thank you for your time. Please help yourself to any of my active projects!
